Overview

Our Garment Customization App allows users to design and personalize apparel with ease. Add logos, graphics, and text to garments, providing a seamless and intuitive experience for creating unique designs.

Features

  • Interactive Interface: Drag and drop elements to customize garments in real-time.
  • Logo Integration: Choose from a variety of logos or upload your own design.
  • Design Tools: Add text, images, and patterns to create a personalized look.
  • Live Preview: See your designs in real-time on the product mockup.
  • Easy Download: Export your customized design with a single click.

Customization Options

  1. Logos: Select from popular brands or upload your own logo for a unique touch.
  2. Graphics:
    • High-quality images and pre-designed elements to enhance your designs.
    • Includes brand logos, patterns, and icons.
  3. Garment Selection: Choose from various garment styles and colors, including T-shirts, hoodies, and more.

How It Works

  1. Select a Product: Start by choosing the type of garment you wish to customize.
  2. Add Elements: Use the design tools to insert logos, graphics, and text.
  3. Preview Design: View your customized garment in real-time.
  4. Download or Print: Save your design or proceed to printing.
