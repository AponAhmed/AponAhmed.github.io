Product Customizer

Garment Image Customization App

Duration: 10 Days | Deployed single-handedly

Overview

Our Garment Customization App allows users to design and personalize apparel with ease. Add logos, graphics, and text to garments, providing a seamless and intuitive experience for creating unique designs.

Live Demo Available:

https://product-customizer-zfmo.vercel.app

Features

Interactive Interface

Drag and drop elements to customize garments in real-time with intuitive controls

Logo Integration

Choose from a variety of logos or upload your own design for personalization

Design Tools

Add text, images, and patterns to create a personalized look for your garments

Live Preview

See your designs in real-time on the product mockup before finalizing

Easy Download

Export your customized design with a single click for printing or sharing

Customization Options

1. Logos

Select from popular brands or upload your own logo for a unique touch to your garments.

2. Graphics

  • High-quality images and pre-designed elements to enhance your designs
  • Includes brand logos, patterns, and icons for versatile customization

3. Garment Selection

Choose from various garment styles and colors, including T-shirts, hoodies, and more.
